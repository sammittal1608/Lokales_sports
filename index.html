How do I book an activity at Lokales?

You can easily book your favorite activities online through our website. Simply select the activity, choose your preferred date and time, and complete your booking. You can also use our Exclusive Point Packs to secure your spot.

What are Exclusive Point Packs and how do they work?

Exclusive Point Packs allow you to purchase sessions in advance at a discounted rate, securing your place in upcoming activities.

Can I bring a friend to my booked session?

Yes, you can bring a friend along. Ensure you both arrive on time for the session to enjoy the activity fully.

What is the difference between the two Lokales branches?

Each branch offers unique activities and experiences tailored to its location and audience. Explore both to find your favorite!

Do I need prior experience to join a class or activity?

No, all our activities are beginner-friendly and guided by experienced instructors.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking through your account on our website or by contacting our support team.
